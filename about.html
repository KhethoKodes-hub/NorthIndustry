North Industry was born out of a passion for artistry and flavour. Founded as a private company on 2 June 2023 and registered with CIPC under number 2023/790630/07.

Who We Are

A creative and quality-driven company specialising in premium pastries and decorative services — dedicated to delivering exceptional taste and visually stunning designs that elevate every occasion.
